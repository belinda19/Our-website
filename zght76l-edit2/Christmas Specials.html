Christmas Specials

Boozy drinks to get you in the holiday spirit!

The North Pole

Ingredients:

  • 4 oz Vodka
  • 2 oz Kahlúa
  • 4 tbsps Chocolate syrup
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract
  • 3 tsps Molasses
  • 1/8 tsp Ginger
  • 1/2 cup Heavy cream or whole milk
Optional: whipped cream candy canes and gingerbread cookies

How to make:

In a cocktail shaker combine the vodka, Kahlúa, chocolate syrup, vanilla, molasses, and ginger. Shake until well combined

Add ice and shake again. Strain into 4 glasses

Top off each glass with heavy cream. Dollop with whipped cream and garnish as desired

Sugared Cranberry Ginger Mimosas

Ingredients:

  • 2-3 oz Ginger ale or ginger beer
  • 2-3 oz Cranberry juice
  • 2-3 oz Champagne
Optional: Sugared cranberries

How to make:

Combine all ingredients in a champagne flute

No need to stir, the bubbles will make sure everything mixes up enough

Top with sugared cranberries!

Moscow Reindeer

Ingredients:

  • Handful of fresh mint or peppermint
  • 2 oz Vodka
  • Juice of 1/2 lime
  • 6 oz Ginger beer
  • Crushed ice
Optional: Crushed and whole candy canes

How to make:

Fill mug or glass with crushed ice and handful of mint. Muddle

Stir in vodka, lime juice and top with ginger beer

Sprinkle crushed candy cane on top and garnish with a whole candy cane

Cranberry Apple Cider Punch

Ingredients:

  • 4 cups Apple cider, chilled
  • 2 cups Cranberry juice cocktail, chilled
  • 2 cups Ginger ale, chilled
  • 12 oz Frozen orange juice
Optional: Apple slices, orange slices, frozen cranberries

How to make:

Combine cider, juice, and orange juice. Refrigerate until serving time

Just before serving pour punch into punch bowl and add chilled ginger ale

Garnish with frozen cranberries, apple & orange slices

White Christmas Mojito

Ingredients:

  • Juice of 1 lime
  • 8 Mint leaves
  • 1 tbsp Sugar
  • 2 tbsps White rum
  • 1 tbsp Coconut rum
  • 1/4 cup Canned coconut milk
  • Sparkling water
Optional: Pomegranate seeds

How to make:

In a glass, muddle the lime juice, sugar and mint leaves until the leaves have broken down. Fill the glass half way with ice

In a blender, combine the white rum, coconut rum, and coconut milk and pulse until smooth. Pour over the ice and stir to combine

Top with sparkling water, mint and pomegranate

Jack Frost

Ingredients:

  • 3 cups Ice
  • 1 cup Pineapple juice
  • 1/2 cup Vodka or light rum
  • 1/2 cup Blue Curacao
  • 1/2 cup Cream of coconut
Optional: Shredded coconut, simple syrup

How to make:

Add ice, pineapple juice, vodka, blue curacao, and cream of coconut to a food processor. Pulse until smooth

Coat rim of glass in simple syrup, and coat with shredded coconut

Pour cocktail into glasses and serve

Dry Martini

Ingredients:

  • 2 1/2 oz Gin
  • 1/2 oz Dry vermouth
  • 1 dash Orange bitters
Optional: lemon twist

How to make:

Add all the ingredients into a mixing glass with ice and stir until very cold

Strain into a chilled cocktail glass

Garnish with a lemon twist

Bloody Kirby

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 oz Grey Goose Le Citron Flavoured Vodka
  • 1 1/2 Kirby cucumber slice, peeled
  • 1 dash Tabasco sauce
  • 1/4 oz Dry Vermouth
  • 1 1/2 oz Tomato juice
Optional: 1 Kirby cucumber spear

How to make:

In a shaker, muddle the vodka, cucumber, tabasco and vermouth. Strain into a rocks glass filled with ice

Top with the tomato juice and garnish with a Kirby cucumber spear

Margarita

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Blanco tequila
  • 1 oz Fresh lime juice
  • 1/2 oz Orange liqueur
  • Agave syrup
Optional: Lime wheel, Kosher salt

How to make:

Add all the ingredients into a shaker with ice, and shake until chilled

Strain into the prepared rocks glass over fresh ice

Garnish with a lime wheel and kosher salt

Strawberry Daquiri

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 oz Aged Puerto Rican or Jamaican rum
  • 3/4 oz Fresh lime juice
  • 1 oz Rich simple syrup
  • 1 Strawberry
Optional: 1 Orchid and cocktail umbrella

How to make:

Add all the ingredients into a blender

Blend until smooth and pour into a Margarita glass

Garnish with an orchid and a cocktail umbrella

Manhattan

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Bourbon or Rye
  • 1 oz Sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitters
  • 1 dash Orange bitters
Optional: Brandied cherry

How to make:

Add all the ingredients to a mixing glass with ice, and stir until well-chilled

Strain into a chilled coupe

Garnish with a brandied cherry

Cranberry Orange Mimosa

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Cranberry juice
  • 1 oz Orange juice
  • Champagne or Prosecco
Optional: Fresh orange slices

How to make:

Add the juice to a champagne flute

Top the glass up with champagne or prosecco

Garnish with a fresh orange slice
